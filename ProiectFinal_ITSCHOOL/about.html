WE ARE MYSKIN

MYSKIN was created in 2019 by our dermatologist, Mihaela, who wanted to better serve her patients with easier access to the treatments their skin needed. From that day through today, our focus is providing the highest quality products and straight-from-the-experts information to guide you through your skin care journey.

IS GOOD SKINCARE ONLY ABOUT SKIN?

We believe that the best skincare is the kind of care that goes deeper than skin. Not just to make us look better, but make us feel and act better. It is care for our well being and self-esteem, care about the choices we make, the values we respect, and care for the environmental footprint we leave. We insist on truly effective ingredients, honest claims, environmentally friendly packaging, care, passion and respect. Because a radiant heart leads to radiant skin.
